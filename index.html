About me

My main domains of expertise are cloud architecture and cloud-based solutions. Since university I have been passionate about them and I have never stopped to study and increase my knowledge. I continuously try to stay up to date with new technologies to design the best solution and simultaneously bring innovation.
Beyond the job title, when needed I also played roles closer to the business such as project and product manager. My versatility together with technical skills have led me to become a tech lead and a point of reference for the management in a short time. I know how to make decisions autonomously, taking responsibility for them, aware of my strengths and resources. I also understood that it is essential that the people around me do the same, feeling free, valued, and motivated. It is essential for me to be able to recognize the responsibilities and not be disheartened by mistakes.
I'm a tech lover but I can also be an old-school guy. I like doing sports and anything artistic indeed I'm passionate about cinema, literature, and photography.
